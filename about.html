About EmployeIntern

Our mission is to connect talented students with meaningful internship opportunities

Our Story

EmployeIntern was founded in 2023 with a simple mission: to bridge the gap between talented students and companies offering meaningful internship opportunities.

We recognized that finding the right internship can be a challenging and time-consuming process for students, while companies often struggle to connect with qualified candidates for their internship programs.

Our platform was built to solve these challenges by creating a dedicated space where students can discover opportunities that align with their skills and career goals, and where employers can find motivated talent to contribute to their organizations.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Meaningful Connections

We believe in creating meaningful connections between students and employers that lead to valuable experiences for both parties.

Inclusivity

We're committed to providing equal opportunities for all students, regardless of their background or circumstances.

Innovation

We continuously innovate to improve our platform and provide the best possible experience for our users.

Integrity

We operate with honesty and transparency in all our interactions with students, employers, and partners.
